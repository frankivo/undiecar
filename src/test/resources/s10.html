Season 10

Welcome to Undiecar Championship season 10!

This season we are sticking with the tried and proven IR05 Indycar, Silverado 2013 truck and Radical SR8 combo we used last season. As always, all cars and tracked used in our main championship are free on iRacing.

The championship will feature nine rounds, three of which will be held on ovals, with the best seven points scoring races to count. So if you miss a week, donâ€™t like that weeks car/track or have a really bad day, you will still be able to have a good result at the end of the season.

Division 2

Anyone with an iRating below 2000 (average of oval/road licenses) at the start of the season will be allowed to gain points in both the main championship and a Division 2 championship. Previous winners of an Undiecar Championship are automatically placed in Division 1. Divisions are calculated based on the official iRacing statistics from the day of the first race of the season (oval driver stats | road driver stats).

Points system

At the conclusion of each race, the top finishers will score points towards the drivers championship, according to the following scale:

1st: 20 points
2nd: 18 points
3rd: 16 points
4th: 14 points
5th: 12 points
6th: 10 points
7th: 9 points
8th: 8 points
9th: 7 points
10th: 6 points
11th: 5 points
12th: 4 points
13th: 3 points
14th: 2 points
15th: 1 points

Bonus points will be awarded for pole position, fastest lap in each event, most laps led in each race and least incidents in each event (must not be more than one lap down on the leader of their class).
